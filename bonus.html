Shanks - Radio Host

Description

Radio show hosts provide a personal connection between the radio station and listening audience and may be recognized for on-air work such as entertaining discussion, interviewing guests, providing news and traffic reports, and setting up music and advertisements.

physical/personality traits

  1. excellent communication and presentation skills.
  2. performance skills and a clear voice.
  3. the ability to generate original ideas.
  4. a personable and confident manner.
  5. a broad range of interests, including current affairs.
